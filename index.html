Rule book

                    Game Setup:

                    Click on the "Start" button to begin the game.
                    You'll see a grid of face-down cards. Memorize the position of each card to match pairs later.


                    Gameplay:

                    Click on any two cards to reveal their faces.
                    If the two cards match, they will remain face-up. Great job!
                    If the two cards don't match, they will flip back face-down. Try again!
                    Continue flipping pairs of cards until all pairs are matched.


                    Tips:

                    Pay attention to the positions of cards when they flip back over. Use this information to strategize your next moves.
                    Try to remember the locations of matching cards to make successful pairs.
                    Keep track of the number of moves you make and try to complete the game with as few moves as possible.


                    Game Completion:

                    Once you've successfully matched all pairs, a congratulatory message will appear.
                    Your total time and number of moves will be displayed. Challenge yourself to beat your own records!


                    Restarting the Game:

                    To play again, simply click on the "Restart" button to reset the game board and start a new game.


                    Have Fun:

                    Enjoy the challenge and have fun testing your memory skills!
